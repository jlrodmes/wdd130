Nicaragua

Nicaragua is country located in Central America, bordered by Honduras to North, Costa Rica to the South, Pacific Ocean to the West and Atlantic Ocean to the East. Nicaragua has many tourisctic destinies, it has an island in the middle of a lake which has 2 Volcanos, many beaches and mountains, rives and lakes. Nicaragua is known as "The land of lakes and Volcanos" with a rich population culture.
