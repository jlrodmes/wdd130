Purpose

WWRROD has more than 75 unique itineraries, we offer travelers more whitewater rafting trips than any other outfitter on the planet. But it is not just about how long we have been in business, or the number of trips we offer. It is really about the WWRROD experience.

Audience

WWRROD trips are designed to allow families and travelers to enjoy the best deal for recreational purpuses. We offer many packages for companies, schools, entities, family and personal who wish to be away from their hectic routine and improve their mental and physical health.
